## 2. Comprehensive Feature Breakdown

### A. Immersive Home Portal (`/`)
- **Cinematic Banners:** Displays high-definition photography of Chhattisgarhi landscapes.
- **Seasonal Experience Cards:** Recommends destinations based on current weather cycles (e.g. highlight waterfalls during heavy monsoons, forest canopies during dry winters).
- **Responsible Tourism Guidelines:** Interactive widgets outlining cultural respect policies.

### B. Smart Explorer & Filtration System (`/explore`)
- **Radial Distance Slider:** Filters destinations located within standard distance bands (e.g. 10km, 50km, 100km) from user's current GPS location.
- **Ecological Carrying Filter:** Identifies conservation zones requiring active eco-protection badges.
- **Adaptive SVG Maps:** Fuses active local storage pending approvals directly onto SVG vector map coordinate points.

### C. Contributor Onboarding & Staging Wizard (`/creator`)
- **Verification API Check:** Simulates verification routines evaluating travel handles across social platforms.
- **Structured Coordinate Form:** Prompts creators to upload names, category badges, WGS coordinates, local history notes, respect guidelines, safety alerts, and gear checklists.
- **Backlog Dispatch Indicator:** Visually maps submission queues with status labels (`approved`, `pending`, `rejected`).

### D. Governance Moderation Deck (`/admin`)
- **Staging Review Backlog:** Displays pending submissions.
- **Accept/Reject Controls:** Administrators can inspect metadata, edit details, and commit the destination directly to the active system index, making it instantly visible on live exploration maps.
- **Ecological Traffic Meters:** Monitors visitor congestion indices across major parks.

---

## 3. Emergency Support SOS Dashboard
- **SOS Dispatch System:** A secure telemetry link dispatching live GPS coordinates to local police and emergency medical networks.
- **Offline Safety Deck:** Displays direct local ambulance contacts, police stations, and forest ranger hubs sorted by radial proximity.
