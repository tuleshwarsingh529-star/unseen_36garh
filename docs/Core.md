# CG Tourism Platform — Core Product Foundation Documentation
## Product Philosophies, Local-First Ecosystem Strategies & User Journeys

---

## 1. Core Platform Purpose
The **CG Tourism Platform** is designed to digitally preserve and showcase the natural landscapes, historical heritage, and tribal traditions of Chhattisgarh. It functions as a sustainable digital tourism operating system that bridges the gap between urban visitors, local guides, and government administration.

## 3. Product Vision & Differentiators
Unlike standard commercial hotel or flight booking applications, the platform serves as a sovereign digital infrastructure platform:
*   **Decentralized creator inputs:** Local guides and forest rangers are the primary authors of destination records, empowering rural micro-economies.
*   **Cultural Authenticity Over Commercialization:** Tribal geometry patterns, historical elder lore, and eco-tourism ethics are highlighted on primary screens to prevent over-tourism and preserve local lifestyles.
*   **Dual Government-Creator Trust model:** Ensures visual reliability using official government approval markers, verified creator badges, and high-contrast UX4G readability.

## 5. Local-First Development Manifest
- **Rural Ingress First:** Design UI containers to fit lightweight packages, minimizing frame drops on budget mobile devices.
- **Eco-Reminders by Default:** Embed respect prompts on place details (e.g. "Do not bring plastics past the cascade security boundary") to establish sustainable travel habits.
